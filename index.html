Catnip Catcher

 Catnip Catcher is a work in progress game that I have been working on solo for the past few months. It is being made in Unity so I am learning C# and the unity editor envrionment as well. Catnip catcher is a 2D overhead perspective game where the player can become one of two different cats. The objective of the game is to grab 15 bags of catnip as one of these cats without losing your 9 lives.

CarGame

 CarGame is a work in progress semester long group project. This is a project for my Virtual Worlds class. This class goes over Unity, C#, and Unity's ECS and DOTS system. CarGame is a wave survival zombie driving game. The player takes control of the main truck character and the goal is to survive as long as possible. The zombies progressively do more damage and get faster and do more damage as time goes on.
